MEMORY GAME

How to Play?

  • When you click the start game button, the cards will shuffle.
  • You will only have 2 seconds to keep in your mind the location of the shuffled cards. Be Careful!
  • You must click the cards in the correct order of the letters in my surname. Correctly following the sequence earns you 100 points.
  • In other words, after the letters are shuffled, you must write ÖZDEMİR by clicking on the letters Ö,Z.D,E,M,İ,R respectively.
  • GOOD LUCK!
