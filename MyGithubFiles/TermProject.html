-At the exciting intersection of music and artificial intelligence, I am working towards both enhancing my technical skills and making a creative contribution. I aim to participate in various projects and research endeavors to further my understanding of this captivating intersection. By increasing my knowledge in this field, I aspire to explore the future potential of integrating artificial intelligence with music.
